Class: module:menu/MenuItem

module:menu/MenuItem(object, prop, setProp, label, options, change, changes, commander)

new module:menu/MenuItem(object, prop, setProp, label, options, change, changes, commander)

Make a selection that determines a single property's value. Defer handling dependent calculations to the next update step, where we will look at a dependency tree to determine what else needs updating. Functionality here is called from button presses. Functionality here is also called from a command pattern (commander).
